The Center for Entrepreneurship's Contact Details and Programs

The Center for Entrepreneurship, a renowned institution for supporting and nurturing entrepreneurial talent, provides essential contact details for those seeking assistance or collaboration. You can reach them at +7 (499) 929-79-27 or email them at contact@cfe.ru. Their office is located at 1-23, The Center for Entrepreneurship. They operate from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The Center for Entrepreneurship offers a range of programs designed to cater to the needs of budding entrepreneurs. Among these programs is the Startup Huddle, which focuses on creating a supportive community for startups. Additionally, their Fasttrac program is aimed at helping aspiring entrepreneurs navigate the challenges of starting a new business.

More About the Center for Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship collaborates with numerous organizations to provide comprehensive support to entrepreneurs. Their Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom program offers hands-on training and practical insights into running a successful business. Moreover, the Scaleup Acceleration Program is designed for businesses looking to scale their operations and achieve significant growth. These programs and more demonstrate the Center's commitment to fostering a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem.
