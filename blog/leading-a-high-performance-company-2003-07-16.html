Key Organizations Involved

The Entrepreneurs' Organization is a global network of entrepreneurs that helps business leaders leverage their skills and resources through shared experiences and peer-to-peer learning. By providing a supportive community, this organization aims to inspire and help entrepreneurs achieve their business goals. Another key organization, the Global Entrepreneurship Week, hosts a series of events and activities to promote entrepreneurial engagement on a global scale. These initiatives provide entrepreneurs with opportunities to learn, connect, and be motivated by successful business practices from across the world.
