Strategies for Growth in Competitive Markets

April 11, 2003

Insights from the 'Growth in Competitive Conditions: Faster, Higher, Stronger' Conference

On April 11, 2003, The Center for Entrepreneurship co-organized the conference, 'Growth in Competitive Conditions: Faster, Higher, Stronger...' Over 70 growth entrepreneurs, consultants, investment professionals, as well as top managers from large companies participated in the conference, focusing on ways to improve company success in increasingly competitive markets. The full-day conference was divided into panel discussions that addressed issues growth companies face, such as growth strategies, implementing these strategies, and raising capital. Dennis Bugrov from McKinsey & Company discussed 'Elements of Successful Growth Strategies,' outlining examples of international companies, such as Gillette, that have remained strong by acquiring and creating new products. Elena Chirkova from Deloitte & Touche expanded upon this theme by discussing diversification, using companies like Revlon and GE as examples. Kirill Dimitriev of Delta Capital Management presented on what private equity funds look for in potential investment targets, highlighting crucial factors for growth entrepreneurs seeking private equity investment. The conference included insightful discussions on market sectors that have received the most direct investments, notably the food and telecommunications sectors.

Keynote Speaker: Dennis Bugrov

Dennis Bugrov is a senior partner at McKinsey & Company and has extensive experience advising companies on growth strategies. His expertise lies in helping firms navigate competitive landscapes by identifying and leveraging growth opportunities. At the conference, he shared valuable insights into how global companies like Gillette have successfully sustained growth through strategic acquisitions and product diversification. His talk emphasized the importance of innovation and adapting to market changes to maintain a competitive edge. With a track record of guiding firms to market dominance, Bugrov is a leading authoritative voice on corporate growth and strategy. He has been instrumental in advising numerous companies across different sectors to achieve and sustain long-term growth.
