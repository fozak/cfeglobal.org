Winner Announced in the 2004 Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest

November 26, 2004

Rouben Vardanyan Wins National Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest

Rouben Vardanyan, President of Troika Dialog Group, emerged as the winner of the national Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest, which was held for the second time by Ernst & Young in 2004. This esteemed contest is an integral component of Ernst & Young's international Entrepreneur Of The Year® Award program. Launched 18 years ago in 1986, the program aims to honor and recognize exceptional entrepreneurial achievements that contribute to the global and national economies.

Key Organizations Contributing to the Success of the Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest

Ernst & Young, a global leader in assurance, tax, transaction, and advisory services, conducts the Entrepreneur Of The Year® Award program annually. EY has been instrumental in supporting and recognizing entrepreneurs who have made significant impacts on the business landscape. Another notable organization involved is Troika Dialog Group, whose President, Rouben Vardanyan, was honored this year. The contest also sees participation from companies like Yevrazkholding, Renessans Kapital, and the agency Mikhaylov and Partners, who support the event through sponsorship and media partnerships. The Center for Entrepreneurship played a pivotal role in organizing and promoting the contest, ensuring it reached a wide array of entrepreneurs and business leaders.
