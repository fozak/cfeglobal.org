Masterboards Startup Huddle Event Summary

November 12, 2020

Masterboards Hosts Successful Startup Huddle

On November 12, 2020, The Center for Entrepreneurship hosted the Startup Huddle event in collaboration with Masterboards. The event, named 'GrowUP', was conducted via ZOOM. The session focused on entrepreneurial strategies and practical knowledge sharing. Attendees had the opportunity to engage with key industry leaders and experts from various fields, including SF Education, Chapay Hub, IBCG, Zamwell and Largo Winch, InBonds, APEX Consulting Group, IRIS, and others. There were special sessions led by Smile Finance, outlining their approach to 'Alternative funding'. The aim was to highlight innovative ways to finance new ventures while mitigating risks. Another key speaker discussed the significance of robust business planning and strategy. One of the program's highlights was a session by Dora.Team focused on effective team collaboration and project management tools. EmotionExpert provided insights into maintaining mental well-being amongst entrepreneurs. Discussions also emphasized the importance of learning, mentorship, and continuous development facilitated through platforms like Telegram.

About Participating Organizations

SF Education offers a variety of online courses and workshops aimed at improving entrepreneurial expertise. Chapay Hub is a co-working space that provides networking and collaboration opportunities for startups. IBCG is known for its comprehensive business consultancy services. Zamwell and Largo Winch excel in investment and funding solutions for new ventures. InBonds specializes in innovative financial instruments. APEX Consulting Group offers tailored consulting services for growing businesses. IRIS focuses on providing digital solutions to enhance business processes. Each of these organizations contributed significantly to the success of the Startup Huddle event, facilitating an environment of shared learning and growth.
