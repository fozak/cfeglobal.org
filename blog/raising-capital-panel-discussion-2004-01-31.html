Raising Capital Panel Discussion

June 2003

Inaugural Entrepreneurship Group Discusses Raising Capital Challenges

The Center for Entrepreneurship held its first entrepreneurship group to discuss raising capital. This ongoing networking and panel event brings together entrepreneurs of growing companies with investment and service sector professionals to discuss topics vital to creating a healthy entrepreneurial culture. The panel included experts from various industries. Kirill Dmitriev, Vice President at Delta Capital Management, discussed what equity funds look for in investment targets and common mistakes entrepreneurs make when seeking investment. Entrepreneur Dmitry Agarunov, owner of GameLand, shared his experience of obtaining private equity investment to fund the expansion of his publishing business. Yuri Mostovoy, CEO of RosBusinessConsulting International, discussed his companyâ€™s recent IPO and the steps entrepreneurs need to take to obtain equity funding. The event generated valuable insights for all attendees and was a stepping stone towards a more collaborative entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Key Organizations Contributing to the Event

Delta Capital Management is a prominent investment firm known for its focus on funding innovative startups and businesses. Their expertise in identifying high-potential investment opportunities has made them a leader in the equity funding space. GameLand, under the leadership of Dmitry Agarunov, is a successful publishing company that has expanded significantly through strategic equity investments. They publish multiple magazines, catering to a diverse audience, and have set a benchmark in the publishing industry. RosBusinessConsulting International, a comprehensive business services provider, successfully completed an IPO, showcasing their robust business model and growth potential. Their insights into equity funding and IPO processes offer practical knowledge to other aspiring entrepreneurs.
