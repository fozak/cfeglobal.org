Center for Entrepreneurship announces new board members

January 28, 2011 - November 14, 2014

Center for Entrepreneurship expands its leadership team

The Center for Entrepreneurship has announced the inclusion of several distinguished individuals to its board of directors, a pivotal move aimed at harnessing an array of expertise and innovation to further its goals. Key appointees include Karen Horn, Chair of the Board; Jenny K. Britell, President of Structured Ventures, Inc.; Steven Pize, President of Deucalion Securities; and Anna Belova, Director of Strategy and Corporate Development at SUEK.

Profiles of Key Board Members

Karen Horn: As the Chair of the Board, Karen Horn brings extensive experience in both leadership and entrepreneurship. She holds a prominent role at the Center for Entrepreneurship and has served as the Vice Chair at the US-Russia Investment Fund and the US-Russia Economic and Legal Development Fund. Her strategic insights are expected to drive the Center towards achieving its mission of fostering entrepreneurial growth.

Jenny K. Britell: Jenny K. Britell is known for her significant contributions to Structured Ventures, Inc., where she serves as Chairman and President. Her expertise in investment and funding will provide invaluable guidance to the Center's financial strategies and initiatives.

Steven Pize: Steven Pize serves as the President and Chair of Deucalion Securities. With a robust background in financial management and strategic planning, Steven's role as Director and Co-Chair at several investment and development funds enriches the Board with essential financial oversight.

Anna Belova: Anna Belova, Vice Chair of the Board, is currently the Deputy General Director and Director of Strategy and Corporate Development at SUEK.
