Franchising: Solutions for Business Development

March 3, 2011

A Closer Look at Franchising for Business Growth

The Center for Entrepreneurship recently held a successful meeting focusing on franchising as a solution for business development. The event, hosted in a business breakfast format, provided a renewed networking opportunity, information exchange, and business insights. Key speakers included Andrey Popov, a business development consultant from Subway, who delved into strategies for creating a successful franchise network. Marya Iljina, the Director of franchising project of Spar Middle Volga, spoke on the dynamics between franchisors and franchisees and common pitfalls to avoid. Michael Trishin, CEO of OOO 'Consult Studio', discussed the development of franchise packages and contrasts between local and international franchises. Lastly, Alexander Skornyakov, Director of Franchise Network of Tea Spoon, shared his leadership experiences in growing a successful franchise network.

About The Center for Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship is dedicated to fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship by providing resources, training, and networking opportunities to budding and established entrepreneurs. They host various programs including Fasttrac, which offers comprehensive entrepreneurial training, and the Scaleup Club, designed to support businesses in their growth phase. Through its seminars, mentoring programs, and conferences, The Center for Entrepreneurship empowers entrepreneurs to innovate and scale their businesses effectively.
