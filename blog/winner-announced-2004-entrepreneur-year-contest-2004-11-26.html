Winner Announced in the 2004 Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest

November 26, 2004

National Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest Winners

Rouben Vardanyan, President of Troika Dialog Group, emerged as the winner of the 2004 Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest, organized by Ernst & Young. The award aims to recognize and honor outstanding entrepreneurial achievements in fostering global and national economies. Some notable winners included Alexander Ladan, Stanislav Kostiashkin, Marina Malyhina, Andrey Fedorov, Alexander Sharonov, Michael Lifshits, Paul Sigal, and Sergey Mordovin.

Key Organizations Recognized for Their Contributions

The Ernst & Young Entrepreneur Of The Year program has been recognizing outstanding entrepreneurs since its inception in 1986. It provides a platform for inspiring business leaders who have significantly contributed to the growth of their enterprises. Troika Dialog Group, under the leadership of Rouben Vardanyan, has consistently played a key role in the investment banking sector. Since 1991, Troika Dialog has been at the forefront of developing financial markets and investment opportunities. The Economist has often featured their strategic business moves and influential market presence. Marina Malyhina of MAGRAM Market Research additionally signifies the role of market research in guiding companies towards data-driven decision-making, showcasing the valuable synergy between research and business strategy.
