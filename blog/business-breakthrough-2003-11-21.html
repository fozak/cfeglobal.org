Key Partner Organizations

The Young Entrepreneurs' Organization, also referred to as YEO, is a global network that connects young and successful entrepreneurs. Through peer-to-peer learning, the organization fosters business growth and personal development. They offer various events and programs, including the European Conference, providing a platform for networking and sharing knowledge among entrepreneurs. My Business Magazine is a premier publication focusing on industries, insights, and innovations within the business world. They provide valuable information, resources, and opportunities to help entrepreneurs and business leaders stay informed and succeed in their ventures.
