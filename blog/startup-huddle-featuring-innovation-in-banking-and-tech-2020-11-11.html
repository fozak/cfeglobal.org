Startup Huddle Featuring Innovation in Banking and Tech

November 11, 2020

Exploring Innovations with Antti Tarakkam and Jarno Voutilainen

Join our next Startup Huddle, featuring exciting presentations from two leaders in their fields: Antti Tarakkam from Bankify and Jarno Voutilainen from Northern Pails. Discover how Bankify is revolutionizing the B2B sector with innovative API solutions that enhance banking experiences for both businesses and their clients. Additionally, explore Jarno Voutilainen's breakthrough work in True Wireless Stereo (TWS) technology and its impact on the audio industry. Don't miss this opportunity to engage with these experts and gain insights on the future of banking and technology.

Key Organizations and Innovations

Bankify aims to disrupt the traditional banking industry by providing seamless and customizable API solutions. Their technology allows financial institutions to integrate a variety of services quickly, enhancing user experience and operational efficiency. Bankify's APIs cover a broad range of functions from user authentication and transaction management to financial analytics and reporting. This flexibility positions them as a crucial player in the B2B fintech space. On the other hand, Northern Pails is making waves in the consumer electronics market, particularly with their advancements in True Wireless Stereo (TWS) technology. Jarno Voutilainen, a key figure at Northern Pails, has led projects that push the boundaries of audio quality, connectivity, and battery life. Their TWS products are known for exceptional sound clarity and innovative features, setting new standards in the industry.
