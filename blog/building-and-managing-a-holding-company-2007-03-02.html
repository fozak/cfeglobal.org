Expanding Your Business: Building and Managing a Holding Company

March 2, 2007

Learning from Business Experts: Building and Managing Successful Holding Companies

Join us for an insightful discussion with notable experts about the intricacies of building and managing a holding company. Key speakers include Oleg Gamazin, President of SynerGo Holding Company, who will delve into the history and legal structure of his holding company, the standards for selecting new companies, and his managerial roles. Additionally, Alexander Pechersky, General Director of ALT Consulting Company, will share proven management models and advice on coordinating activities between managing and daughter companies. Finally, Vassili Rudomino, Senior Partner at ALRID Law Firm, will discuss legal considerations and antimonopoly issues related to holding companies. The event will provide valuable insights for entrepreneurs aiming to build and manage diversified business structures effectively.
