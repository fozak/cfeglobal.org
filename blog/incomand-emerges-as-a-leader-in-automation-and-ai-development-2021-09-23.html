Incomand Emerges as a Leader in Automation and AI Development

September 23, 2021

Incomand's Journey in Automation and AI

Incomand has established itself as a major player in the automation and AI sectors. Founded in 2020, Incomand quickly made a name by developing innovative B2B solutions. The company focuses on automating routine business operations and delivering cutting-edge SaaS products. By participating in various industry events and leveraging initiatives like Startup Huddle, Incomand continues to expand its influence and network. In the words of their founders, their mission is to simplify business processes through intelligent automation. They have set an ambitious goal to become the leading AI company by 2025. Key projects include CRM systems, process automation, and integrated AI solutions, driving the new wave of digital transformation.

Key Collaborations and Insights

Numerous industry leaders and startups have partnered with Incomand to further their automation capabilities. Notable collaborations include CRAFT Systems, renowned for its advanced modular solutions, and KnoDL, a pioneer in data exchange solutions. These partnerships enable Incomand to offer a broad spectrum of services, from intuitive web platforms to sophisticated IoT applications. Additionally, Incomand continues to participate in prominent industry conferences and workshops, sharing their expertise and gaining insights into the latest technological trends. Their commitment to innovation ensures they remain at the forefront of AI development, making them a vital player in the evolving tech landscape.
