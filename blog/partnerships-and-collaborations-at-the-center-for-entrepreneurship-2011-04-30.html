Partnerships and Collaborations at The Center for Entrepreneurship

April 30, 2011

Exploring Strategic Collaborations and Partnerships

The Center for Entrepreneurship proudly partners with numerous organizations to support and amplify growth and innovation among entrepreneurs. One such collaborator is Ernst & Young. Together, we sponsor and manage various projects, including the highly prestigious Entrepreneur of the Year competition. Another key partner is Delovaya Rossia, a membership group for mid-sized business leaders. We work closely on research and event initiatives tailored to these entrepreneurs' needs.

Engaging with Key Organizations

We also collaborate with Russian Microfinance Center (RMC), which provides a national cooperation forum among government bodies, society, and investors. OPORA, another major partnership, focuses on organizing significant conferences and seminars for small and medium-sized businesses. Our association with My Business magazine allows us to provide robust informational and marketing support for our seminars and conferences. Moreover, our relationship with the Center for Entrepreneurship and Executive Development (CEED) fosters networking and partnership opportunities between local and South East European entrepreneurs. Lastly, we serve as a key advisor to the Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO), having played an instrumental role in establishing their local chapters.
