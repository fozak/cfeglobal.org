Entrepreneurs as Leaders in Business and Society

August 20, 2016

Annual Conference Focuses on Business Leadership and Society Impact

The Center for Entrepreneurship is holding its annual conference 'Entrepreneurs as Leaders in Business and Society'. The event will provide entrepreneurs with a platform to discuss best practices in leading their company's growth, including how to use information technology, personnel management, raising capital, and the role entrepreneurs play in creating jobs, national wealth, and developing society. The event will be conducted in English with simultaneous interpretation. It highlights three powerful attributes of leadership - authenticity, trust, and optimism - those that inspire people to follow leaders. Key Organizations Involved

Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Program recognizes leaders who drive sustainable growth in their businesses. Their objective is to unite and engage with the entrepreneurs driving successful businesses, celebrating their achievements across various categories. Another notable organization is Cyprus Investment Promotion Agency (CIPA), which aims to enhance and promote Cyprus as an attractive international investment destination. They provide support and necessary information to potential investors aiming to establish or expand their businesses. The conference is supported by various strategic partners, sponsors, and information partners, ensuring that entrepreneurs gain valuable insights and networking opportunities.
