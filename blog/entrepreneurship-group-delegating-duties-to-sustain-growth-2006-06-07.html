Entrepreneurship Group: Delegating Duties to Sustain Growth

June 7, 2006

Strategies for Effective Growth Management

The Center for Entrepreneurship and My Business Magazine hosted an event on June 7, 2006, focusing on how entrepreneurs and hired managers can sustain fast growth through effective duty delegation. Notable speakers included entrepreneur Dmitry Agarunov, owner of (game)land; service sector expert Ekaterina Ryazantseva, managing partner of Anderson Partners; and hired general manager Valery Lukovnikov, general manager of New Idea Investment Group.

Key Organizations and Their Roles

My Business Magazine is renowned for publishing insightful content for entrepreneurs, including the famous My Business magazine, which caters to a wide readership of business owners and managers. Anderson Partners is a significant consultancy firm providing various services to enhance business efficiency, while New Idea Investment Group is prominent in funding innovative business ideas across multiple sectors. Together, these organizations contribute to fostering an environment where entrepreneurship can thrive and develop sustainably.
