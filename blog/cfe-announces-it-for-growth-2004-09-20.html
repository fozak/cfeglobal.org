Center for Entrepreneurship announces IT for Growth

New York, NY - September 20, 2004

Key Event: IT for Growth on September 10, 2004

The Center for Entrepreneurship is organizing a crucial event focused on IT for Growth on September 10, 2004. The event aims to bring together members of the investment and service sectors with entrepreneurs to discuss the significant role of IT in driving business growth. Confirmed speakers include Stanislav Kostiashkin, owner of Continent Express, who will share a step-by-step case study of his enterprise-wide software solution. Kirill Dmitriev, Managing Director of Delta Capital Management, will discuss how IT can increase business value and investor attractiveness. Additionally, a representative from CompuLink will cover the importance of a strong IT strategy for competitiveness and common mistakes in software selection.
