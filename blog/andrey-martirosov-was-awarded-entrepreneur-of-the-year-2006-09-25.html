Andrey Martirosov was awarded Entrepreneur of the Year

September 25, 2006

Achieving Entrepreneurial Excellence: Andrey Martirosov of UTair Honored

The General Director of airline UTair, Andrey Martirosov, has been named the national winner of the international competition Entrepreneur of the Year, 2005 organized by Ernst and Young. Andrey Martirosov, having headed airline UTair since 1999, has brought back the enterprise from bankruptcy and has transformed it into an industry leader. In 2006, the company plans to earn 400 million rubles net profit, four times more than the 2005 figure. In June 2006, Mr. Martirosov will represent at the international Entrepreneur of the Year competition in Monte-Carlo.
