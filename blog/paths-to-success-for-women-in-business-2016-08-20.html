Paths to Success for Women in Business

August 20, 2016

Empowering Women Through Networking and Learning

This conference is designed to bring together business and professional women to increase business knowledge and motivation by being engaged in networking and learning surrounded by like-minded individuals. Participants will have the opportunity to make connections, build relationships, create opportunities, and find a better work-life balance in an interactive and creative atmosphere.

Key Organizations Supporting the Event

Ernst & Young is a multinational professional services network and one of the largest audit firms in the world. The Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Program celebrates the achievements of outstanding entrepreneurs who demonstrate vision, leadership, and success. DD Atelier is a renowned fashion brand that focuses on designing and producing tailored attire for women, promoting empowerment through fashion. Additionally, the U.S. Chamber of Commerce is a key partner, working to support and advocate for businesses of all sizes across industries.
