Mergers & Acquisitions: An Instrument for Company Growth

October 5, 2008

Expert Insights on Mergers & Acquisitions for Business Growth

Entrepreneur Salim Tharani, CEO of Gallery Out of Home Media, shared his insights on why he prefers M&As over organic growth, critical factors considered during an M&A, the criteria used for selecting an M&A target, post-M&A integration, HR strategy post-deal, and evaluating the short-term and long-term success of acquisitions. Entrepreneur Igor Buchshtab, Director of LYNX, presented a case study on defending his company from a hostile takeover and offered advice to entrepreneurs on protecting their businesses. Expert Elena Lomakina, Partner at Ernst & Young, discussed the different types of M&As, provided typical examples, shared M&A deal strategies, outlined due diligence steps, and highlighted the risks involved. Organizational Development Expert Mark Rozin, President of Ecopsy, emphasized the importance of integrating corporate cultures and reducing employee resistance to M&As. Media Expert Anton Smirnov, Chief Editor of the Mergers & Acquisitions magazine, gave an overview of M&As in the media sector and successful media strategies.

Key Organizations Supporting Mergers & Acquisitions

Ernst & Young is a global leader in assurance, tax, transaction, and advisory services. Their comprehensive M&A services include strategic advice, due diligence, valuation, and post-transaction integration. LYNX and Gallery Out of Home Media are notable for their roles in M&As, showcasing the application of strategies discussed. For further insights into corporate culture and organizational development post-M&A, Ecopsy offers expert consultation services. Mergers & Acquisitions magazine provides in-depth analysis and coverage on the latest trends, deals, and strategies within the M&A space.
