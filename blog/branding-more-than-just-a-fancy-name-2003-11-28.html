Branding – More Than Just a Fancy Name!

November 28, 2003

Insights on Branding Success and Challenges

The Center for Entrepreneurship recently hosted a highly informative event focusing on the importance and challenges of branding under the theme Branding – More Than Just a Fancy Name! Key speakers included industry leaders who shared their unique insights and experiences. Notable speaker Oleg Tinkov, Chairman of the Board at Tinkoff Ltd, discussed how his company achieved branding success, the value it added, and shared growth strategies. Tatyana Lifantyeva, President of LBL Media Advertising Agency, elaborated on brand creation, market research, and the intricate process of introducing and repositioning a brand. From an investor's perspective, Paul Price and Natalie Polischuk of Delta Capital Management delved into the financial implications of branding, highlighting costs and profitability trade-offs.

Key Organizations Making an Impact

Tinkoff Ltd, known as one of the leading banks and online financial ecosystems, has garnered substantial success due to its innovative branding efforts. Led by Oleg Tinkov, the company has revolutionized the way financial services are marketed and consumed, demonstrating the power of effective branding. LBL Media Advertising Agency, managed by Tatyana Lifantyeva, serves a diverse range of high-profile clients including Bistroff, Danone, and Ahmad Tea. Their expertise in market research and brand tactics has solidified their reputation as a premier advertising agency. Delta Capital Management, with key figures Paul Price and Natalie Polischuk, plays a crucial role in the investment sector by educating entrepreneurs on the financial significance of branding. Their insights into the costs and benefits of branding contribute significantly to business growth and investor confidence.
