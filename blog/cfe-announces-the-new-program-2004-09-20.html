Center for Entrepreneurship Announces New IT for Growth Program on September 10, 2004

2004-09-20

The Center for Entrepreneurship's IT for Growth Program

The Center for Entrepreneurship invites you to participate in our upcoming event, 'IT for Growth', on September 10, 2004. This program is part of our Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom. Join us as we bring together members in the investment and service sectors with entrepreneurs of growing companies to discuss how IT can drive growth and productivity.

Our confirmed speakers include: Stanislav Kostiashkin, owner of Continent Express, who will share a step-by-step case study on how he leverages IT to enhance his corporate travel services business. From the investment industry, Kirill Dmitriev, Managing Director of Delta Capital Management, will discuss the role of IT in increasing business value and investor appeal. From the service sector, Mikhail Lyastch, President of CompuLink, will talk about the competitive edge bustling companies can gain through effective IT strategies, common mistakes in selecting software, and optimizing IT costs.

Key Speaker Highlight: Stanislav Kostiashkin

Stanislav Kostiashkin, the owner of Continent Express, is a pioneering entrepreneur in the corporate travel services sector. With his profound understanding of IT and its pivotal role in business growth, Stanislav implements innovative solutions to maintain a competitive edge. Through a detailed case study, he will walk attendees through the recent adoption of an enterprise-wide software solution in his company. This strategic IT move aims to enhance productivity and operational efficiency, crucial factors for sustaining rapid business expansion. Beyond his accomplishments at Continent Express, Stanislav's insights and practical experiences will provide invaluable learning for entrepreneurs aiming to harness the full potential of IT in their own ventures.
