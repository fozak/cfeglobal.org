About the Organizations

The Young Entrepreneurs' Organization, now known as Entrepreneurs' Organization, is a global network of business owners fostering entrepreneurship through peer-to-peer learning and leadership development. The organization hosts Global Entrepreneurship Week and various other initiatives aimed at supporting business leaders. Vasily Visokov is a notable figure, known for his chairmanship at Center-Invest Ltd., a major regional bank focusing on sustainable banking practices. These testimonials from prominent business figures highlight the impactful role The Center for Entrepreneurship plays in fostering business growth and entrepreneurship.
