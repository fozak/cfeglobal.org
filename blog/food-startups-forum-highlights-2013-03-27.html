FOOD STARTUPS Forum Highlights

March 27, 2013

Insights and Trends at FOOD STARTUPS Forum

The Center for Entrepreneurship is excited to announce the FOOD STARTUPS Forum, taking place at the restaurant-bar 'Projector'. The event aims to bring together various food projects, mentors, and investors in the food industry. Attendees will gain insights into the current trends, analytics, and case studies of both Russian and international food startups. The event showcases presentations from leading food industry experts and offers networking opportunities for entrepreneurs looking to scale their ventures. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity to connect with key players and stay up-to-date with the latest industry developments.

Key Organizations and Partners

One of the highlighted programs at the Forum is the Global Entrepreneurship Week, which promotes entrepreneurial initiatives worldwide. Another noteworthy entity is Ernst & Young, renowned for their Entrepreneur of the Year program that recognizes exceptional business talents. The event is organized by STARTUP WOMEN, a partner of the Alliance for Entrepreneurship Development, which supports and empowers female entrepreneurs across various industries. These organizations play a vital role in fostering innovation and growth within the entrepreneurial ecosystem, providing resources and mentorship to budding startups.
