Mission and Management of The Center for Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship (CFE) is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial growth and supporting business development. Managed by a highly experienced team, CFE offers a range of programs designed to aid startups and established businesses alike. Our main areas of focus include providing mentoring services, organizing Startup Huddle, and facilitating connections through the Scaleup Club. The CFE is proud to support entrepreneurs at various stages of their journey, offering programs like the Fasttrac and the Scaleup Acceleration Program.

Key Organizational Partners

US Russia Business Council: An organization focused on enhancing US-Russia commercial relations. The council serves as a vital bridge for businesses looking to engage in trade and investment opportunities. AlphaSniffer, LLC: Led by Misha Plam, this innovative company specializes in cybersecurity solutions, providing cutting-edge technology to protect enterprises from digital threats. Center for International Private Enterprise (CIPE): CIPE supports good governance and democratic growth through fostering economic freedom and entrepreneurship worldwide. Foundation for Russian American Economic Cooperation (FRAEC): FRAEC aims to promote economic partnership and cooperation between American and Russian companies, focusing on creating a collaborative business environment.
