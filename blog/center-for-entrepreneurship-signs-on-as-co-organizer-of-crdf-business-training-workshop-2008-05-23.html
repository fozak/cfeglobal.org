The Center for Entrepreneurship Signs on as Co-organizer of CRDF Business Training Workshop

May 23, 2008

The Center for Entrepreneurship Partners with CRDF for Business Training Workshop

During the four-day workshop, 25 science and technology (S&T) entrepreneurs will attend CRDF and The Center for Entrepreneurship (CFE) designed training and mentoring sessions to prepare each for future CRDF-organized U.S. business introductions. CRDF has provided extensive business training opportunities to a broad network of S&T entrepreneurs for more than a decade. Its support and partnership have promoted functional and lasting collaborations, encouraging the development of new U.S. business opportunities. As a leading organization advancing entrepreneurship, CFE’s experience is a welcome addition to CRDF’s work, CRDF Senior Vice President Dr. Eric Novotny said. Technological innovation is one of the S&T field’s competitive advantages and CFE looks forward to working with CRDF in promoting technology entrepreneurship as a way to diversify the emerging economy, notes Executive Director of CFE. More information about the workshop and CRDF’s entrepreneurship and innovation-focused work can be found at www.crdf.org/industry. To learn more about CFE’s initiatives to accelerate entrepreneurship in the region, please reference www.cfe.ru. Source: CRDF. Back My Business Magazine, Media Partner OPORA. More about Key Organizations

CRDF, or the Civilian Research and Development Foundation, has been dedicated to fostering cross-border collaboration, innovation, and business development through various training programs and workshops. Their mission is to create opportunities that advance science and technology by involving entrepreneurs, researchers, and industry leaders. CRDF’s work focuses on enhancing the capabilities of S&T entrepreneurs, providing them with the tools and knowledge needed to succeed internationally. Their extensive network and resources have made them a prominent figure in the realm of business training and mentorship.OPORA is an association of entrepreneurial organizations committed to improving the business environment for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). By advocating for policy changes and providing support services, OPORA plays a critical role in promoting entrepreneurship and economic growth. Their efforts ensure that entrepreneurs have a voice and the necessary resources to thrive in a competitive landscape. Combining forces with other organizations, OPORA actively contributes to the creation of a robust entrepreneurial ecosystem.
