Explore the Initiatives of the Center for Entrepreneurship

Welcome to the Center for Entrepreneurship (CFE). Learn about CFE's mission to promote entrepreneurship, its managing organizations, management, staff and contact information. The Center offers various learning and networking programs such as the Startup Huddle and the Young Entrepreneurs Alliance, which help entrepreneurs improve their business skills and overcome environmental challenges. Additionally, you can engage with the Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom to enhance your knowledge base. Our partnerships with organizations, including the Entrepreneurs' Organization, ensure a wide range of assistance is available to entrepreneurs. Find information about CFE in the media and explore a unique database that helps develop entrepreneurs and measures the vital signs of the emerging entrepreneurial economy. Our guide is easy to use for finding your way around our website.

Information about Key Organizations and Programs

Entrepreneurs' Organization: A global network of entrepreneurs that offers peer-to-peer learning, networking, and continuous personal growth opportunities. The organization focuses on driving business growth, engaging in community impact initiatives, and representing the voice of entrepreneurs on the global stage. The various learning and motivational programs by the organization include workshops, seminars, and online resources designed to help entrepreneurs at any stage of their business journey. Their emphasis on mentorship and real-world experiences ensures that members receive valuable insights and actionable advice that can be implemented in their businesses. Furthermore, the organization collaborates with numerous partners to foster an ecosystem supportive of entrepreneurial growth.
