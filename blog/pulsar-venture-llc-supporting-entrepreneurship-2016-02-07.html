February 7, 2016

Pulsar Venture LLC: A Key Player in Entrepreneurial Development

Pulsar Venture LLC, established in 2009, has become a crucial part of the innovative ecosystem, specializing in strategic and investment consulting. With 12 in-house and 20 independent experts, the company offers services in business strategy, deal structuring, financial management, and investor relations. Pulsar Venture also aids foreign companies and investors entering the market and supports fundraising efforts for entrepreneurial projects. The firm's goal is to bridge the gap between invention and project implementation, fostering skill development and entrepreneurial competence.

Additional Key Services and Collaborations

Pulsar Venture LLC is dedicated to building a sustainable entrepreneurship ecosystem. Its experts provide comprehensive support to startup teams in the high-tech sector. Among the services offered are smart packaging of business for investors and mentorship in development stages, from seed to startup. The company maintains strong partnerships with various entities, including techno-parks, universities, and venture funds. Their collaboration with the The Center for Entrepreneurship started in 2012, and they have since carried out numerous projects together, significantly contributing to the global entrepreneurship landscape and providing invaluable resources for budding entrepreneurs.
