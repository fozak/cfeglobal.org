Business Angels: Investing Your Money in Start-up Companies

November 23, 2006

Expert Insights on Investing in Start-ups: A Comprehensive Guide

The Center for Entrepreneurship hosted a memorable event on November 23, 2006, centered around the theme: Business Angels: Investing Your Money in Start-up Companies. This gathering provided valuable insights and networking opportunities for participants. Notable panel speakers included Oleg Manchulyanstev, President of RespublikaIDEI, Lawrence Wright, General Director of Xlerate Technologies, and Dionis Gordin, Partner at Private Capital and a member of the National Network of Business Angels.

Key Organizations and Partnerships

The event partnered with multiple reputable organizations to ensure a comprehensive discussion on start-up investments. One key contributor was OPORA Russia, the Association of Entrepreneurial Organizations, known for its commitment to supporting small and medium businesses. Also, the event included a detailed overview of Fasttrac, a program designed to help aspiring entrepreneurs develop their business plans, understand their market potential, and sharpen their strategic thinking. Additionally, the Creative Marketing Group and Agency of Internet Technologies ltd. played crucial roles in the event's organization and promotion, further fostering the entrepreneurial ecosystem.
