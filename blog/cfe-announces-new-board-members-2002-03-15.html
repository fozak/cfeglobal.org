Center for Entrepreneurship Announces New Board Members

Washington DC, March 15, 2002

Center for Entrepreneurship Welcomes Esteemed Professionals to Board of Directors

The Center for Entrepreneurship is proud to announce the addition of several esteemed professionals to its Board of Directors. These new members bring a wealth of experience and expertise, further strengthening the organization's mission to foster entrepreneurship and innovative business practices. Patricia M. Cloherty has been appointed as Chairman of the Board of Directors. Ms. Cloherty is a renowned venture investor, known for her extensive efforts in promoting entrepreneurship globally. She also serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors for The U.S. Russia Investment Fund. Jenne K. Britell joins the board as a Member. She is currently the Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc., bringing significant leadership and business acumen to the team. Steven L. Pease, a General Partner at Deucalion Securities, also joins as a Board Member. His diverse expertise in finance and business development is a valuable addition. Additionally, Richard G. Turner, former Executive Vice President of South Shore Bank, has been named to the Board. He brings a strong background in banking and organizational management to the Center.

Backgrounds and Contributions of Key Members

The Center for Entrepreneurship is honored to have such distinguished individuals on its Board of Directors. Let's take a closer look at the key members who have joined us. Patricia M. Cloherty - A leading figure in the venture investment world, Patricia M. Cloherty has played an instrumental role in promoting entrepreneurship on a global scale. She has a rich history of initiating and supporting programs aimed at developing entrepreneurial activities internationally. Her expertise and vision are expected to significantly enhance the Center's impact. Jenne K. Britell - As the Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc., Jenne K. Britell brings a wealth of knowledge in structuring complex business ventures. Her experience spans various leadership roles, making her a pivotal addition to the Center's governance. Steven L. Pease - With a dynamic career in finance and business development, Steven L. Pease adds profound financial acumen to the Board. As General Partner at Deucalion Securities, his insights will be invaluable in driving the Center's strategic initiatives. Richard G. Turner - With an extensive background in banking, Richard G. Turner brings vital expertise to the Center. His previous role as Executive Vice President at South Shore Bank equips him with a deep understanding of organizational management and financial operations, which will greatly benefit the Center's goals.
