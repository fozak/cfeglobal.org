Center for Entrepreneurship Announces Global Entrepreneurship Week Dates

February 15, 2024

Key Dates and FAQs for Global Entrepreneurship Week

Global Entrepreneurship Week, the world's largest celebration of innovators and job creators, will take place from November 18 to 24 in 2024. This annual event inspires individuals through various local, national, and global activities designed to help them along their entrepreneurial journey. Offering everything from large-scale competitions and events to intimate networking gatherings, GEW connects participants with potential collaborators, mentors, and investors, introducing them to new opportunities and possibilities. As announced by the Global Entrepreneurship Network, the dates for the upcoming years are: 2024 - November 18 to 24, 2025 - November 17 to 23, 2026 - November 16 to 22. Since its inception in 2008, when 77 countries participated, the number of countries celebrating GEW has grown to approximately 180 each year. Participants of all backgrounds are encouraged to join GEW events, share ideas, and explore opportunities, enriching the entrepreneurial ecosystem globally.

Why You Should Get Involved

Hosting or participating in an event during Global Entrepreneurship Week offers significant benefits. For organizations, it raises awareness and gains recognition for your work year-round. It also helps you reach new audiences, identify potential volunteers or new employees, and strengthen connections with existing supporters and sponsors. For aspiring entrepreneurs, it provides a platform to connect with potential collaborators, mentors, and investors. The Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and US Department Of State are key organizations that support GEW, offering various programs and initiatives to help entrepreneurs thrive. The Global Entrepreneurship Network provides resources, events, and advocacy aimed at making it easier for anyone, anywhere to start and scale a business. They also offer platforms like the Global Entrepreneurship Week, which serves as a crucial touchpoint for aspiring and established entrepreneurs to connect and grow.
