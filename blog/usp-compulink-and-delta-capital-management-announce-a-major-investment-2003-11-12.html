USP Compulink and Delta Capital Management Announce a Major Investment

November 12, 2003

Delta Capital Investment Fuels USP Compulink's Expansion

Delta Capital Management, manager of The US Russia Investment Fund, announced a significant investment in USP Compulink. This investment enables USP Compulink to expand its business to meet the growing needs of the IT market. USP Compulink has been active in the IT market since 1993 and was recognized in 2002 as one of the fastest-growing IT services companies. The IT market is expected to grow significantly, driven by the need for large companies to leverage IT for operational efficiency and the emergence of growing medium-sized companies that require IT systems for management. USP Compulink offers a strong mix of system integration and high value-added consulting services. In 2002, they won significant contracts in the program 'Electronic 2000-2010.' The company aims to become a leading national one-stop shop for IT services through expanding its high value-added services and geographic reach. The investment is also expected to facilitate the company's entry into the IPO market within a few years. This move is anticipated to benefit both market and state, resulting in the formation of a strong middle class.
