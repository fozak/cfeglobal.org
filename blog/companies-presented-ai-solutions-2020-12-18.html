Companies Presented AI Solutions at Startup Club Meeting

December 18, 2020

Innovative AI Solutions at Startup Club Meeting

On December 17, the Startup Club held a meeting dedicated to projects leveraging AI technologies. Eight innovative projects were presented. Pavel Krivozubov, the Head of Robotics and AI, welcomed participants and highlighted the relevance of AI in today's rapidly transforming world. Denis Didkovsky, Chair of the Startup Club and Director of The Center for Entrepreneurship, underlined the effectiveness of the Club as a platform for expert assistance and investment opportunities. Dmitry Yagodkin moderated the session, showcasing projects like the Intelligent Pedestrian Crossing by Kirill Minibaev and Smart Timber by Tatiana Eliseeva. Other noteworthy projects included Alexey Cyplakov's IPG system for airport slot coordination, Vasiliy Manko's AURORAi for monitoring technical objects, and Svetlana Efimova's Oz Forensics for advanced biometric platforms. Artem Artemov introduced B2NLP, a language processor for creating valuable knowledge from text, while Maxim Shevchenko presented the Digital Russia platform to solve business queries. Laurent Akopyan from iPavlov shared the journey from startup to a mature company, highlighting five key products, including a chatbot platform and neurocryptography solutions.

Key Organizations and Their Roles

The Center for Entrepreneurship aims to foster entrepreneurship through actionable programs and initiatives. The institution provides resources for startups, from initial ideas to scaling businesses. Key initiatives include the Acceleration Programs and Mentoring Programs, offering comprehensive guidance and support to emerging businesses. With a focus on innovation and collaboration, the Center bridges the gap between entrepreneurs and investors, fostering a dynamic ecosystem for growth and success. By participating in events like the Startup Club, The Center for Entrepreneurship connects members with critical insights and opportunities.
