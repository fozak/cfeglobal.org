Customized Programs for Entrepreneurial Success

May 1, 2011

The Center for Entrepreneurship Offers Tailored Programs to Enhance Business Knowledge

The Center for Entrepreneurship offers customized worldwide programs that improve an entrepreneurs functional business knowledge and develop skills important for company success. These programs cover areas such as entrepreneurial marketing and business planning. Additionally, they help entrepreneurs overcome environmental challenges that hinder company growth, such as protecting intellectual property, utilizing the legal system, and approaching capital providers. The Center utilizes a robust delivery methodology, including focused courses, workshops, peer learning events, and networking opportunities. These programs foster not only entrepreneur-to-entrepreneur but also mentor-to-entrepreneur, entrepreneur-to-investor, and entrepreneur-to-policymaker learning. By bringing together all the players that influence entrepreneurial success, the Center offers unique, integrated, and experiential learning programs.

Key Organizations in Entrepreneurial Support

Among the key organizations supporting entrepreneurial growth is Ernst & Young, which sponsors the prestigious Entrepreneur of the Year program. This program recognizes entrepreneurs who demonstrate excellence and extraordinary success in areas such as innovation, financial performance, and personal commitment to their businesses and communities. Fasttrac, one of the programs under the Center, is a leading educational initiative that provides entrepreneurs with the tools to build their businesses. It includes a series of workshops and courses that cover critical aspects of business management, market research, and strategic planning. Moreover, the Entrepreneurship Groups offer a platform for continued learning and peer networking, enabling participants to share experiences and insights, thus fostering a collaborative community of like-minded individuals dedicated to entrepreneurial success.
