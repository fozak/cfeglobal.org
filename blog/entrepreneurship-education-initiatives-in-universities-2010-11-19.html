Entrepreneurship Education Initiatives in Universities

November 19, 2010

Highlighting Best Practices in Entrepreneurship Education

This conference organized by The Center for Entrepreneurship and supported by the MIRBIS business school will be held as a signature event for Global Entrepreneurship Week. The conference will highlight best practices in entrepreneurship education in universities.

Key Organizations Supporting Entrepreneurship Education

Global Entrepreneurship Week is an initiative that introduces entrepreneurship to young people in six continents. Founded by the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and the UKs Enterprise Campaign, this week-long event inspires millions to explore entrepreneurial activities. Another crucial supporter, MIRBIS business school, offers high-quality business education and is known for its international orientation. The Center for Entrepreneurship consistently organizes such initiatives, supporting the development of a robust entrepreneurial ecosystem.
