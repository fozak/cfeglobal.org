Board of Directors Announcement

Meet the Board of Directors

Patricia Cloherty, Chairman – Ms. Cloherty is the Chairman of The Investment Fund, and a renowned venture investor who has initiated and supported a wide range of programs to encourage entrepreneurship internationally.
Jenne Britell – Member of the Board of Directors, Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc.
Mikhail Kupriyanov - Partner and Director of Business Development at SYSCOM (leading manufacturer of LCD monitors and portable Braille computers).
Steven L. Pease – Member of the Board of Directors, General Partner at Deucalion Securities.
Richard G. Turner – Member of the Board of Directors, Former Executive Vice President at South Shore Bank.
