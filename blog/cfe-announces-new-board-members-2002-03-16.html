Center for Entrepreneurship Announces Leadership Changes

Washington DC, March 15, 2002

Center for Entrepreneurship Introduces New Board and Senior Management

The Center for Entrepreneurship has announced significant updates to its board of directors and senior management team. These changes are expected to enhance the center's mission of fostering entrepreneurship globally. The new board includes distinguished leaders from various sectors: - Patricia M. Cloherty, Chairman, Board of Directors, is a renowned venture investor who has initiated and supported numerous programs to encourage entrepreneurship internationally. - Sergey Borisov, President, Association of Entrepreneurial Organizations. - Jenne K. Britell, Chairman and CEO, Structured Ventures, Inc. - Steven L. Pease, General Partner, Deucalion Securities. - Richard G. Turner, Former Executive Vice President, South Shore Bank. The center also thanked Victor Sedov, Member Emeritus, for his years of dedicated service.

Key Biographies

Patricia M. Cloherty: Patricia M. Cloherty is widely recognized for her groundbreaking efforts in the field of venture capital and entrepreneurship. As Chairman of the Board of Directors, she brings extensive experience and a global perspective to the Center for Entrepreneurship. Over the years, Cloherty has been instrumental in launching numerous initiatives aimed at nurturing startup ecosystems around the world. Her leadership and vision have been key drivers in fostering cross-border collaborations and investments. Previously, Cloherty served as Chairman and CEO of several venture capital firms, making significant contributions to various high-growth sectors. Her commitment to empowering entrepreneurs and fostering innovation is evident in her prolific career and numerous accolades. Key initiatives led by Cloherty include international conferences and mentorship programs designed to bridge knowledge and resources between seasoned investors and budding entrepreneurs. Her strategic approach ensures that the Center for Entrepreneurship remains at the forefront of global entrepreneurial advancements.
