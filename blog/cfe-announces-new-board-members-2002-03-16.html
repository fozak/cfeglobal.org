Center for Entrepreneurship Announces New Board of Directors

Washington DC, March 15, 2002

New Board of Directors Appointed to Guide the Center for Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship is pleased to announce its newly appointed Board of Directors, a diverse group of seasoned professionals dedicated to fostering entrepreneurship and innovation. The board is comprised of Patricia M. Cloherty (Chairman), Jenne K. Britell, Steven L. Pease, and Richard G. Turner. These distinguished individuals bring a wealth of experience and a profound commitment to developing programs that support entrepreneurial ventures.
