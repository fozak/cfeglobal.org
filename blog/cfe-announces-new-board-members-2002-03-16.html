Center for Entrepreneurship Announces Key Leadership Changes

Washington DC, March 15, 2002

Center for Entrepreneurship Announces Key Leadership Changes

The Center for Entrepreneurship is pleased to announce new appointments within its leadership team. These changes aim to strengthen and enhance the organization's commitment to fostering entrepreneurship internationally.

Key Members of the Center for Entrepreneurship Team

Patricia M. Cloherty is the Chairman of the Board of Directors. She is a renowned venture investor who has championed various programs to support entrepreneurship globally. Sergey Borisov serves as the President of the Association of Entrepreneurial Organizations. Jenne K. Britell is a Member of the Board of Directors and also the Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc. Mikhail Kupriyanov is a Partner and Director of Business Development at SYSCOM. Steven L. Pease is a General Partner at Deucalion Securities. Richard G. Turner is a Former Executive Vice President at South Shore Bank.
