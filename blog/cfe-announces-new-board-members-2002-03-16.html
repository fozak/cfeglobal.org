New Board of Directors Announced at U.S. Russia Investment Fund

Washington DC, March 15, 2002

Introducing the Diverse Board Members

Patricia M. Cloherty, Chairman of the Board, leads the newly announced Board of Directors at The U.S. Russia Investment Fund. Accompanied by a distinguished group of professionals:
Sergey Borisov, President of Association of Entrepreneurial Organizations of Russia; Jenne K. Britell, Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc.; Mikhail Kupriyanov, Partner and Director at SYSCOM; Steven L. Pease, General Partner at Deucalion Securities; Richard G. Turner, Former EVP of South Shore Bank.
