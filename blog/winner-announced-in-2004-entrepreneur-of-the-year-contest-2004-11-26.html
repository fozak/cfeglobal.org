Winner Announced in the 2004 Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest

November 26, 2004

Rouben Vardanyan Wins Entrepreneur Of The Year 2004 in National Contest

Rouben Vardanyan, President of Troika Dialog Group, has won the national Entrepreneur Of The Year Contest in 2004. This annual contest is part of Ernst & Young's international Entrepreneur Of The Year Award program. Established 18 years ago, the program aims to recognize and honor outstanding entrepreneurial achievements in developing the global and national economies.

About Ernst & Young and Troika Dialog Group

Ernst & Young is one of the largest professional services networks in the world, offering audit, tax, consulting, and advisory services to companies across multiple industries. The organization is renowned for its commitment to building a better working world by providing insights and quality services that foster trust and confidence in capital markets and economies globally.

Troika Dialog Group, led by President Rouben Vardanyan, is a leading private investment house. Under Vardanyan's leadership, the group has grown significantly, helping to shape the investment landscape by providing financial advisory, asset management, and brokerage services. Troika Dialog is known for its innovative solutions and strategic partnerships, playing a crucial role in the financial sector.
