Business Angels and Innovators Fair – Capital To Innovations

July 2, 2004

The Center for Entrepreneurship Sponsors Key Fair

The Center for Entrepreneurship was the General Sponsor of 'Business Angels and Innovators Fair – Capital To Innovations' which took place on April 29-30. The Center provided free 'Making Presentation Skills to Investors' training to participants of the fair. For more information about the fair, you may visit the official website.

About the Involved Organizations

The Center for Entrepreneurship is a renowned organization aimed at nurturing entrepreneurial spirit and providing comprehensive support services to entrepreneurs and startups. It offers various programs like the Global Entrepreneurship Week and Startup Club, designed to foster innovation and business growth. The Center plays a pivotal role in inspiring future business leaders by offering top-tier training, mentoring, and networking opportunities. One of the Center’s notable programs includes the Scaleup Acceleration Program, which aids businesses in scaling their operations and accelerating growth. The organization's commitment to entrepreneurship is evident through its extensive array of services and events tailored to meet the diverse needs of the entrepreneurial community.
