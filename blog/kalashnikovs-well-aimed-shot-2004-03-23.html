March 23, 2004

An Entrepreneur's Remarkable Journey to Success

Andrey Kalashnikov, a young businessman, is the winner of the Business Breakthrough competition, a joint project of My Business magazine and the Center for Entrepreneurship. In less than four years, starting from scratch, he built a manufacturing company with a turnover of US $1.2 million.

Spotlight on Key Organizations

My Business magazine is a prominent publication dedicated to supporting and celebrating entrepreneurship. The magazine provides valuable insights and resources for business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs, covering a wide range of topics including business strategy, innovation, and personal development. Through its various initiatives and collaborations, My Business magazine plays a crucial role in fostering a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem and recognizing outstanding business achievements.
