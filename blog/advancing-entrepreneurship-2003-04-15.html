Advancing Entrepreneurship

April 15, 2003

Empowering Entrepreneurs with Education and Networking

The Center for Entrepreneurship (CFE) is a catalyst organization advancing the role and scope of entrepreneurship through a combination of education and network learning activities that target the needs of growth entrepreneurs. CFE's education modules are specifically developed for entrepreneurs who have both the desire and ability to expand their growing companies, and who seek additional knowledge to attain this goal. This knowledge may include how to find sources of investment, prepare a business plan, and present to investors. It may also include developing the skills entrepreneurs need to further their company's success, such as delegation, leadership, and recruiting and retaining high-quality talent. CFE invites entrepreneurs to participate in our policy and network activities that bring entrepreneurs together with other entrepreneurs, policymakers, and investors to discuss and learn about such topics as how to navigate the tax system, ensure financial transparency, and improve corporate governance. Network learning programs are interactive and can take the form of guest speaker forums, entrepreneur exchanges, panel presentations, and conferences. CFE is an initiative of The U.S. Russia Investment Fund, a leading private equity fund, and managed in cooperation with the Foundation for Enterprise Development, a foremost educational organization dedicated to building high-performing entrepreneurial organizations.

Key Organizations Involved

The U.S. Russia Investment Fund (TUSRIF) is an essential organization advocating private equity investments in emerging markets. Its key role in promoting entrepreneurship includes equity investments, loans, and supporting economic development initiatives. Managed by Delta Private Equity Partners, TUSRIF has a diversified portfolio spanning several sectors. The Foundation for Enterprise Development (FED) is another critical body, focusing on educational programs and resources that support the growth and sustainability of entrepreneurial ventures. The FED offers a range of programs and has contributed significantly to policy advocacy and the development of new business models and strategies. By collaborating with these organizations, CFE ensures that its members receive quality education, access to vital networks, and opportunities to engage in impactful discussions with policymakers and investors.
