Highlights from the Startup Huddle Online Event

The latest Startup Huddle online event showcased some exciting innovations on December 25, 2020. Attendees experienced presentations on 3D-modeling and other cutting-edge technologies. One notable solution was the call-center application, ZvonoBot. This SAAS product automates outbound calls, making the process more efficient and reliable. The ZvonoBot platform's capabilities include voice call services, which enhance communication efficiency and effectiveness. It's designed to transform how call-centers operate, covering all aspects from technical to management matters. Another highlight was the language learning platform, Polygled. Polygled integrates social media aspects to encourage language learning among users. The platform supports interactions among learners, building a community focused on language exchange. With an emphasis on educational tools that aid in communication skills, Polygled has become a popular choice among educators and learners. Developers also demonstrated how the platform aids in the growth and development of personal language skills.
