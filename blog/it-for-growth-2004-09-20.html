IT for Growth

September 20, 2004

How Information Technology Drives Business Growth & Productivity

The Center for Entrepreneurship has organized a special event focusing on the crucial role of IT in driving growth and productivity for businesses. The event brings together industry leaders and entrepreneurs to discuss vital IT strategies. Confirmed speakers include Stanislav Kostiashkin, owner of Continent Express, who will elaborate on how IT helps him manage and grow his fast-expanding enterprise. He will present a detailed case study of his current enterprise-wide software implementation. Additionally, Kirill Dmitriev, Managing Director at Delta Capital Management, will discuss how IT increases a business's value and investor appeal. Lastly, Mikhail Lyastch, President of CompuLink, will highlight how IT strategies can enhance competitiveness and efficiency, sharing valuable insights on software selection and optimization.

Key Organizations & Participants

Continent Express is a prominent corporate travel service company that leverages advanced IT solutions to streamline operations and enhance productivity. Delta Capital Management, a leading private equity firm, focuses on increasing the value of portfolio companies through strategic IT innovations. CompuLink provides cutting-edge IT solutions designed to boost business efficiency and drive growth. These organizations collaborate to create a robust entrepreneurial ecosystem, promoting knowledge sharing and strategic implementation of IT to achieve sustained business success.
