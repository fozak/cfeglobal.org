Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom Launch Event on March 12, 2019

On March 12, 2019, The Center for Entrepreneurship will host the launch event for the Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom (DEC). The event will begin at 11:45 AM and will include a keynote speech by Raymond M. Smilor, a prominent figure in the field of entrepreneurship. DEC aims to provide a comprehensive education program focusing on the practical aspects of entrepreneurship, offering a unique opportunity for aspiring entrepreneurs to learn from experts and gain real-world experience.

About the Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom and Key Organizations

The Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom (DEC) is designed to foster entrepreneurial skills through hands-on learning and expert mentorship. This program is part of The Center for Entrepreneurship's ongoing efforts to support and grow the entrepreneurial ecosystem. DEC sessions will cover various topics such as business planning, market analysis, and financial management. The program also includes interactive workshops and networking opportunities with successful entrepreneurs. In addition to The Center for Entrepreneurship, the event will feature insights from key organizations and partners such as the Entrepreneurs' Organization and other notable institutions that play a pivotal role in promoting entrepreneurship and innovation globally.
