Outsourcing for Growth: Minimizing Risks and Increasing Efficiency

February 29, 2008

Maximizing Business Efficiency Through Outsourcing

Entrepreneur Irina Nikiforova, President of Zimaletto, will focus on why her company decided to use outsourcing, the criteria used in choosing partners, steps in outsourcing, controlling quality when outsourcing, and efficiencies gained when using outsourcing. Service Provider Inna Kachalova, Lawyer at Pepeliaev, Goltsblat & Partners will discuss the legal aspects of outsourcing; contracts; outsourcing activities subject to licensing; responsibility of an outsourcing company; and methods to ensure they deliver. Consultant Vyacheslav Lev, Associate Partner, and Natalia Oparina, Partner at Top-Management Consult, will discuss outsourcing myths, pluses and minuses of outsourcing, how to estimate if outsourcing is economically justified, how to minimize outsourcing risks, and will give advice on personnel outsourcing.

About our Key Organizations

Zimaletto is a renowned company known for its innovative approaches in the fashion industry. Pepeliaev, Goltsblat & Partners is a leading law firm providing comprehensive legal support in the field of outsourcing. Top-Management Consult offers expert consulting services for businesses aiming to optimize their operations through outsourcing. These organizations bring a wealth of experience and knowledge that can help other businesses navigate the complexities of outsourcing and achieve substantial growth.
