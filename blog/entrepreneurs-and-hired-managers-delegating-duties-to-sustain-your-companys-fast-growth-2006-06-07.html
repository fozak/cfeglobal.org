Entrepreneurs and Hired Managers Delegating Duties to Sustain Your Companys Fast Growth

June 7, 2006

Moscow Entrepreneurship Group: Entrepreneurs and Hired Managers Delegating Duties to Sustain Your Companys Fast Growth

The Center for Entrepreneurship and Svoi Business magazine hosted the event on June 7, 2006. The topic was: Entrepreneurs and Hired Managers: Delegating Duties to Sustain Your Companys Fast Growth. Panel speakers included Entrepreneur – Dmitry Agarunov, Owner of (game)land; Service Sector – Ekaterina Ryazantseva, Managing Partner at Anderson Partners, and Hired General Manager – Valery Lukovnikov, General Manager at New Idea Investment Group.

Key Organizations in Focus

Svoi Business magazine is a leading publication that covers various aspects of business development and entrepreneurship. Anderson Partners is renowned for its consulting services, particularly in the area of business strategy and management. New Idea Investment Group focuses on fostering innovative ideas and investments in the business sector. Additionally, Ernst & Young organizes the prestigious Entrepreneur Of The Year award, recognizing outstanding entrepreneurial achievements globally. UTair, a major airline discussed during the event, has shown remarkable growth under the leadership of Andrey Martirosov, who successfully transformed the company into an industry leader. These organizations play a critical role in shaping the business landscape and supporting entrepreneurial endeavors worldwide.
