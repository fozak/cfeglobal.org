Center for Entrepreneurship Announces New Board of Directors

September 25, 2006

A Fresh Leadership Perspective

The Center for Entrepreneurship is proud to announce the appointment of a new Board of Directors. This distinguished group brings a wealth of experience and expertise across various industries, promising to guide the Center towards a future of innovation and growth. Richard G. Turner, the new Chairman of the Board, previously served as Executive Vice President at South Shore Bank. Joining him is Jenne K. Britell, a board member at the U.S. Russia Investment Fund and Chairman and CEO of Structured Ventures, Inc. Another notable addition is Steven L. Pease, General Partner at Deucalion Securities, reinforcing the board's robust financial acumen.

Highlight: Richard G. Turner's Leadership

Richard G. Turner has a career that spans several decades, defined by his exceptional leadership in banking and finance. His tenure at South Shore Bank showcased his adeptness at navigating financial landscapes and fostering economic growth. Turner's strategic vision was instrumental in South Shore Bank’s expansion and stability, making him a valuable asset to the Center for Entrepreneurship. His focus on sustainable financial models and community-oriented banking has garnered Turner numerous accolades in the finance sector. With his deep-rooted understanding of financial systems and commitment to entrepreneurial growth, Turner’s chairmanship is highly anticipated. His leadership marks a pivotal step forward for the Center as it embarks on new projects and initiatives to support budding entrepreneurs and innovative businesses.
