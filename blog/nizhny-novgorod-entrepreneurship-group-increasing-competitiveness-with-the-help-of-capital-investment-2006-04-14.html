Nizhny Novgorod Entrepreneurship Group: Increasing Competitiveness with the Help of Capital Investment

April 14, 2006

Strategies to Enhance Market Position Through Capital Investment

Entrepreneur Andrey Mladentsev, General Director of Nizhpharm company, will discuss his successful experience in attracting capital investments to boost Nizhpharm's market position and share lessons learned. Additionally, Sergey Ivanushkin, General Director of AVK Investment company, will highlight the advantages of capital investments over other forms of investment during different stages of company development. He will also discuss the investor's role in managing the enterprise and ensuring owner security in investment deals. Finally, Kendrick White, Managing Principal of Marchmont Capital Partners, will offer insights into the role of investment funds in increasing enterprise competitiveness and the importance of consulting firms in enterprise-investor relations.

About Partner Organizations

AVK Investment company, led by Sergey Ivanushkin, is a prominent investment firm focused on providing capital funds across various stages of company development. With a strong emphasis on strategic partnerships and thorough market analysis, AVK Investment aids businesses in securing essential funds to drive growth and enhance market presence. Nizhpharm company, under the leadership of Andrey Mladentsev, is a notable player in the pharmaceutical industry. The company has consistently demonstrated prowess in utilizing capital investments to fortify its market stance and ensure consistent product innovation. On the other hand, Marchmont Capital Partners specializes in consulting services that bridge the gap between enterprises and investors. Led by Kendrick White, the firm excels in providing business solutions that bolster competitiveness and optimize enterprise-investor collaboration.
