Key Organizations Supporting Entrepreneurs

The Global Entrepreneurship Network is a catalyst for startups and aspiring entrepreneurs. With an expansive reach across 180 countries, it offers programs, events, and tools to help entrepreneurs start and scale businesses. The Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation focuses on providing educational resources and tools for entrepreneurship. It aims to create an enabling environment for businesses through research and policy recommendations. Additionally, the US Department of State partners with organizations worldwide to promote entrepreneurship as a means of economic development and international stability.
