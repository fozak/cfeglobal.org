Partner Organizations and Initiatives

Young Entrepreneur Organization is a global, peer-to-peer network of more than 14,000+ influential business owners with 204 chapters in 61 countries. This organization is dedicated to transforming the lives of entrepreneurs and business leaders around the world by offering a range of programs and events designed to enhance the leadership skills of its members. Meanwhile, the Association of Women Entrepreneurs provides a valuable platform for women entrepreneurs to connect, learn, and grow their businesses. This organization focuses on empowering women through education, advocacy, and networking opportunities. Additionally, OPORA (Association of Entrepreneurial Organizations) plays a crucial role in fostering a business-friendly environment by providing support, resources, and a robust advocacy platform for small and medium-sized enterprises. 'My Business Magazine,' as the Center's media partner, works to highlight success stories and provide insightful articles to guide and inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs.
