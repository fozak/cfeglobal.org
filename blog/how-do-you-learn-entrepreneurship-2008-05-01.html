How Do You Learn Entrepreneurship?

May 1, 2008

Practical Tips on Learning Entrepreneurship

Executive Director of the Entrepreneurs Organization suggests picking up the necessary knowledge from people with practical experience who have taken the hands-on route to building their own companies. This hands-on approach helps budding entrepreneurs to understand real-world challenges and opportunities in the business world. The Scaleup Acceleration Program encourages this method by connecting new entrepreneurs with experienced mentors through various workshops and events.

Key Organizations Supporting Entrepreneurship

The U.S. Russia Investment Fund has been pivotal in promoting entrepreneurship. Founded by Patricia M. Cloherty, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Delta Private Equity Partners, the fund works tirelessly to foster a strong entrepreneurial ecosystem. On March 17, 2008, President of the Federation signed a decree awarding the Order of Friendship to Patricia M. Cloherty for her major contribution to the development of business and for strengthening cooperation between nations. Another key player is the Entrepreneurs Organization, which offers a variety of programs and initiatives to help aspiring entrepreneurs launch, grow, and scale their businesses.
