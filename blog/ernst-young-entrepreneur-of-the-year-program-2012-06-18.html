Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Program

June 18, 2012

Recognizing and Celebrating Successful Growth Entrepreneurs

The Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Awards recognize and celebrate successful growth entrepreneurs. Through the selection process and a formal event for recognizing the winning entrepreneurs, this program builds positive awareness towards entrepreneurship. The Center for Entrepreneurship has been the lead sponsor and has assisted Ernst & Young in delivering and judging this program. We nominate entrepreneurs from all over to participate in this awards program. This is an extremely important program as it recognizes and celebrates role models of entrepreneurs who have overcome adversity to build successful companies. Holding up role models for others to emulate is a proven way to accelerate entrepreneurship in a region.

Key Organizations Involved

Ernst & Young is one of the largest professional services networks in the world, providing assurance, tax, consulting, and advisory services to companies across various sectors. The FastTrac program by the Kauffman Foundation offers training to aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, helping them gain the skills necessary to launch and grow their businesses. Additionally, the Global Entrepreneurship Week is an international event that encourages innovation and entrepreneurship, bringing together aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, policymakers, and thought leaders to foster entrepreneurial growth worldwide.
