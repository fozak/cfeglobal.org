About Key Organizations

Global Entrepreneurship Network (GEN) aims to provide everyone with the opportunity to start and scale a business. Through a platform of programs in 170 countries, GEN leverages data, networks, and initiatives to fuel healthier entrepreneurship ecosystems around the world. Global Entrepreneurship Week is one of the signature programs hosted by GEN, galvanizing more than 10 million people each November through a series of events and activities. These initiatives help educate aspiring entrepreneurs, celebrate success, and foster connections between innovators and startup communities. Similarly, the G20 Young Entrepreneurs Alliance is a collective of organizations across G20 countries dedicated to developing entrepreneurship among young people. This network amplifies the voice of young entrepreneurs and implements critical policies supporting their growth and development.
