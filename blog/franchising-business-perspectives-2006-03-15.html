Franchising – Business Perspectives in Nizhny Novgorod

March 15, 2006

Conference-Exhibition on Franchising in Nizhny Novgorod

On March 15, 2006, the Nizhny Novgorod Regional Ministry for Small Business Support and Development, the Nizhny Novgorod Retailers' Association, and the Center for Entrepreneurship co-hosted a franchising fair/conference. Supported by the Russian Franchising Association and Consulting group Store for Prepared Businesses, the event showcased the potential of franchising as a robust business model.

Key Organizations Supporting the Event

The Russian Franchising Association aims to foster the development of franchising systems and enhance the business environment for franchise enterprises. It is pivotal in uniting franchisors and franchisees, promoting a cooperative network to ensure business success. Consulting group Store for Prepared Businesses specializes in offering franchising opportunities, helping entrepreneurs find and acquire franchises. It assists in the preparation and development of franchise documents, market analysis, and connecting investors with viable business models. These organizations play a crucial role in shaping the future of franchising in the region, providing essential support and guidance to new and existing franchise businesses.
