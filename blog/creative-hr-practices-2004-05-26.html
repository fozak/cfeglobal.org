Creative HR Practices

May 26, 2004

Insights on Creative HR Practices in Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship is proud to present an ongoing networking and panel event aimed at bringing together members in the investment and service sectors with entrepreneurs of growing companies. Hosted on May 26, 2004, this month's topic focuses on Creative HR Practices. Distinguished speakers included Dmitry Agarunov, Owner, Gameland, Kirill Dmitriev, Vice President, Delta Capital Management, and Galina Melnikova, General Director, HR Partners. They shared innovative approaches to attract and retain key staff, conflict resolution strategies between investors and owners, and comprehensive HR function development.

Key Organizations Discussed

Delta Capital Management is a renowned private equity firm that provides strategic investment management solutions. Kirill Dmitriev, Vice President of Delta Capital Management, emphasized how private equity funds can successfully retain top managers and entrepreneurs in their portfolios. He highlighted the common disputes that arise between investors and owners and explained various conflict resolution techniques. HR Partners, led by General Director Galina Melnikova, specializes in providing strategic HR solutions to various organizations. Melnikova shared insights into HR function development, strategy development and implementation, legal and corporate compliance, and executive coaching. HR Partners is notable for its comprehensive approach to motivation and career management, making it a significant player in the HR service sector.
