Comprehensive Learning and Support for Entrepreneurs

The Center for Entrepreneurship offers customized worldwide 'best practice' programs that improve an entrepreneur's functional business knowledge and develop skills important for company success in areas such as entrepreneurial marketing, negotiating/conflict resolution, and decision-making. In addition, our programs help entrepreneurs overcome environmental challenges that hinder company growth, such as protecting intellectual property, utilizing the legal system, and approaching capital providers. The Center utilizes a robust delivery methodology, including: focused courses; workshops; peer learning events; networking opportunities; entrepreneur exchanges; and business plan competitions. These programs foster not only entrepreneur-to-entrepreneur, but also mentor-to-entrepreneur, entrepreneur-to-investor, and entrepreneur-to-policymaker learning. By bringing together all of the players that influence entrepreneurial success, The Center offers unique, integrated and experiential learning programs.
