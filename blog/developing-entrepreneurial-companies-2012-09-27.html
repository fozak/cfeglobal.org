Developing Entrepreneurial Companies

September 27, 2012

Annual Conference on Developing Entrepreneurial Companies

The Center for Entrepreneurship held its annual conference Developing Entrepreneurial Companies. The event provided entrepreneurs with a platform to discuss best practices in leading their company's growth, including how to use information technology, personnel management, raising capital, and the role entrepreneurs play in creating jobs, national wealth, and developing society.

Key Organizations and Participants

Global Entrepreneurship Week's President, Jonathan Ortmans, discussed global best practices in creating conditions for fostering small and medium businesses. Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation's Senior Fellow shared experiences in developing cultures that embrace entrepreneurship. The event also featured talks and case studies by CEOs like Andrey Korneev of Research Center Bioform and Patrick van der Pijl of Business Models Inc. Their insights spanned topics from mastering sales in international markets to optimizing business models for growth and innovation.
