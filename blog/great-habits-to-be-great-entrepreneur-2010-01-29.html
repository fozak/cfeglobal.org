Great Habits to Be Great Entrepreneur

January 29, 2010

Join the Workshop by Verne Harnish on Effective Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship, in collaboration with the Entrepreneurs' Organization, was privileged to host a workshop entitled 'Great Habits to Be Great Entrepreneur. Growth Tools' featuring renowned speaker and author Verne Harnish. In this workshop, Harnish shared valuable insights on team-building, management, and motivation strategies, which are essential for any entrepreneur aiming for greatness. This event, part of the Scaleup Acceleration Program, was a significant opportunity for attendees to enhance their entrepreneurial skills.

About the Collaborating Organizations

The Entrepreneurs' Organization (EO) is a global network of entrepreneurs dedicated to helping small and large business owners achieve their full potential through peer-to-peer learning and connections. EO provides invaluable resources and support, fostering a thriving community where members can share their experiences and gain insights from each other. Another key partner, Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Program, recognizes and celebrates the accomplishments of exceptional entrepreneurs who inspire others with their vision, leadership, and achievement. This program provides not only recognition but also access to a valuable network that can help entrepreneurs grow and scale their businesses. Their combined efforts with The Center for Entrepreneurship aim to provide a comprehensive platform for entrepreneurial development and success.
