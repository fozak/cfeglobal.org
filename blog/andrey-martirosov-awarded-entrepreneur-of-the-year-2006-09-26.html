Andrey Martirosov was awarded Entrepreneur of the Year

September 26, 2006

Andrey Martirosov, General Director of UTair, Recognized for Leadership and Achievements

Andrey Martirosov, the General Director of airline UTair, has been honored as the national winner of the international competition Entrepreneur of the Year 2005, organized by Ernst & Young. Under his leadership since 1999, UTair has recovered from bankruptcy and evolved into an industry leader. In 2006, the company aims to achieve a net profit of 400 million rubles, quadrupling its 2005 performance. Mr. Martirosov will represent at the international Entrepreneur of the Year competition in Monte-Carlo. The event also celebrated other notable leaders in business, including Alexander Ladan, Evgeny Chichvarkin, Stanislav Kostiashkin, Marina Malyhina, Andrey Fedorov, Alexander Sharonov, Michael Lifshits, Paul Sigal, and Sergey Mordovin.

Key Organizations in Entrepreneurial Development

Ernst & Young hosts the Entrepreneur of the Year competition to recognize and honor outstanding entrepreneurial achievements worldwide. Established 18 years ago, the program supports and encourages business leaders who contribute significantly to global and national economic development. The Center for Entrepreneurship is dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial spirit and growth through various initiatives, including the Global Entrepreneurship Week and the Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom. These programs offer valuable resources and opportunities for startups and established businesses looking to scale. The organization collaborates with industry leaders to provide insights and best practices, ensuring the continued success and competitiveness of enterprises.
