Manager & Owner: How to Separate Power

July 1, 2004

Conference and Workshop for Business Leaders

The conference objective is to provide for knowledge sharing among owners, directors, and top managers of leading companies on issues related to the separation of ownership from management, and the implementation of corporate governance best practices across businesses. The event is a collaboration between Vedomosti and the National Independent Directors Association (NIDA) with the participation of The Center for Entrepreneurship. For more information and to register, please visit the provided links.

Key Organizations

The Vedomosti is a reputable business newspaper known for its comprehensive coverage of economic and financial topics. It serves as a vital resource for business leaders who seek in-depth analyses and insights into global and local markets. The National Independent Directors Association (NIDA) plays a crucial role in promoting best practices in corporate governance. They focus on providing directors with essential training and resources, helping them navigate the complexities of business management and ownership. Another notable entity is the Scaleup Acceleration Program, designed to support high-potential businesses in scaling their operations effectively through strategic guidance and mentorship.
