Global Entrepreneurship Week by The Center for Entrepreneurship

November 15, 2010

Join the Global Movement to Celebrate Entrepreneurship

The Center for Entrepreneurship is thrilled to co-host the Global Entrepreneurship Week in partnership with Junior Achievement. Each November, millions of young people from 100 countries participate in tens of thousands of events aimed at joining a growing movement of entrepreneurial people to generate new ideas and to seek better ways of doing things. This week-long event connects young people everywhere through local, national, and global activities to explore their potential as self-starters and innovators. Students, educators, entrepreneurs, business leaders, employees, non-profit leaders, government officials, and many others participate in a range of activities both online and face-to-face to harness the energy of social networking, connect people taking action in their local and global communities, and celebrate entrepreneurship across the globe.

Key Organizations Supporting Entrepreneurship

Kauffman Foundation is a U.S.-based organization that innovates in entrepreneurship and education. They are known for their comprehensive research and ability to support startup funding and educational programs. Enterprise U.K. is another pivotal player, which works towards promoting entrepreneurial culture in the U.K. through various initiatives like Global Entrepreneurship Week. The goal of these organizations is to empower young entrepreneurs by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed. Their contributions to initiatives like Global Entrepreneurship Week result in a powerful, collaborative effort to foster innovation and economic growth worldwide.
