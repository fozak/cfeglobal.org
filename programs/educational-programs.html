Key Programs and Benefits

The Center for Entrepreneurship delineates the entrepreneurial journey in distinct phases: IDEATE, POSITION, COMMIT, REFINE, and LAUNCH. Through the IDEATE phase, get started making your idea a reality and exploring the entrepreneurial journey. In the POSITION phase, focus on market positioning, understanding customers, and solidifying your business model. The COMMIT phase ensures you are ready to reach your market, make sales, and protect your business. The REFINE phase perfects your strategy before launch and ensures your financial readiness. Finally, the LAUNCH phase prepares you for leading and growing your business. The Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom program, implemented since 2006, has trained over 500 higher education teachers across 18 cities, resulting in over 80 new programs and initiatives in more than 30 universities. This intensive 3-5 day seminar employs methods from leading experts to enhance teaching skills and entrepreneurial education. Additionally, the Center's partnerships with organizations like the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and the US Department of State amplify the impact and resources available to aspiring entrepreneurs.
