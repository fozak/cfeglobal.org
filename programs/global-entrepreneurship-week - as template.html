Empowering Entrepreneurs Worldwide Through Global Entrepreneurship Week

Global Entrepreneurship Week: Celebrating Entrepreneurial Spirit

Global Entrepreneurship Week is a massive campaign to celebrate and empower entrepreneurs in every country and community around the world. Each November, 10 million people take part in activities that inspire and provide them with knowledge, experience, and connections to succeed.

Empowering Innovators and Visionaries

Global Entrepreneurship Network operates programs like Global Entrepreneurship Week to make starting and scaling a company accessible to everyone. Supported by partners like Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and organizations such as FedEx, GEN's initiatives focus on celebrating entrepreneurs, enabling ecosystems, and fostering connections worldwide.
