Center for Entrepreneurship Growth Programs

The Center for Entrepreneurship's Role in Scaling Up Businesses

Since 2002, the Center for Entrepreneurship has been the backbone of numerous growth programs for entrepreneurs. The Center offers high-impact programs that have proven to be efficient and effective for entrepreneurs. One such program is the ScaleUp Acceleration Program. This accelerator program is a great way to scale up and start running quickly by providing expert guidance, mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities that can help your business reach its full potential.

Benefits and Opportunities

The ScaleUp Acceleration Program is designed for active entrepreneurs with high growth potential, running for 9 months and including 10 interactive training sessions, at least 10 networking events, and 18 individualized mentor meetings. Participants benefit from guidance and support, knowledge and skill development, networking opportunities, personal growth, problem-solving and innovation, moral and emotional support, long-term business growth, and enhanced innovation and creativity. This mentor-mentee relationship fosters mutual learning and growth, significantly enhancing the chances of building a sustainable business. Partners such as the Global Entrepreneurship Network and the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation contribute to these transformative experiences, ensuring access to global best practices and industry standards.
