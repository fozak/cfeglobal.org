ScaleUp Accelerator Program from The Center for Entrepreneurship

Maximize Business Growth with Expert Support and Resources

Even successful entrepreneurs need support in scaling up the business. The Center for Entrepreneurship has been implementing growth programs for entrepreneurs since 2002. Among these, the ScaleUp Accelerator Program is an excellent way to scale up and run quickly. They provide expert guidance, mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities that can help your business reach its full potential. The ScaleUp Accelerator is designed as a nine-month program for active entrepreneurs with high growth potential. Participants undergo group training and practical training, including 10 interactive training sessions, at least 10 networking events, and at least 18 meetings with an individually selected mentor. The ScaleUp Program is a comprehensive set of tools aimed at both increasing individual business performance and developing the local entrepreneurial ecosystem as a whole.

Additional Information on Programs and Benefits

The ScaleUp Accelerator Program includes training that covers a variety of areas essential for business growth, such as strategic planning, marketing, digital marketing, sales management, personnel management, management and leadership, cash flow management, and finance for non-financiers. Additionally, the program offers unique opportunities for experience exchange through mini-conferences that bring together entrepreneurs, professional service providers, and investors. Participants gain valuable insights into business development issues and expert sessions on resolving problematic situations. The mentoring component encourages individual interaction with experienced entrepreneurs who share their knowledge and help solve practical business challenges. Other benefits of the program include access to international entrepreneurial initiatives, expanding contacts with business organizations, and the expert community at various levels. These elements combine to make the ScaleUp Accelerator Program a vital resource for entrepreneurs seeking rapid growth and long-term success.
