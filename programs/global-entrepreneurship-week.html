Celebrating Global Entrepreneurship and Empowering Worldwide Innovators

The Center for Entrepreneurship: Global Impact

Global Entrepreneurship Week is a massive campaign to celebrate and empower entrepreneurs in every country and community around the world, removing barriers and empowering all individuals, especially those facing structural obstacles or new to the idea of starting a business. With 10 million participants across 200 countries engaging in diverse activities, this initiative inspires individuals to venture into entrepreneurship, providing them with knowledge, experience, and connections essential for success. It envisions an inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem that transcends traditional startup hubs and welcomes participation from all backgrounds and regions.

Enabling Entrepreneurial Success Globally

Powered by the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and supported by a network of strategic partners, Global Entrepreneurship Week fosters long-lasting connections and collaborations. The campaign facilitates access to resources, capital, and opportunities for aspiring entrepreneurs, creating a global platform for celebrating, inspiring, and guiding individuals along the entrepreneurial path. Through a diverse range of activities and partnerships, this initiative plays a crucial role in strengthening local ecosystems and supporting entrepreneurial ventures worldwide.
