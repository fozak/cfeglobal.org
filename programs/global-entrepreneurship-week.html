Celebrating Global Entrepreneurship Week: Empowering Entrepreneurs Worldwide

Helping Entrepreneurs Thrive in a Diverse Global Ecosystem

Global Entrepreneurship Week is a massive campaign that celebrates and empowers entrepreneurs from all corners of the world. With 10 million participants engaging in over 40,000 activities across 200 countries, this initiative aims to inspire, educate, and connect individuals who dream of launching their startups.
