Global Entrepreneurship Week - Celebrating Global Entrepreneurship and Empowering Entrepreneurs Worldwide

Entrepreneurial Initiatives and Success Stories Unleashed

The Center for Entrepreneurship propels a worldwide movement to celebrate and empower entrepreneurs, transcending barriers and fostering success across diverse communities. Global Entrepreneurship Week gathers 10 million people from 200 countries in a week-long celebration with 40,000 activities, competitions, and events. Entrepreneurs, including those facing structural obstacles, are provided with the knowledge, experience, and connections to thrive in their endeavors. From smaller cities in emerging economies to underrepresented communities, GEW envisions an inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem open to all.

Driving Entrepreneurship Beyond Boundaries

The Global Entrepreneurship Week, powered by the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and supported by an array of strategic partners, including JA Worldwide and Endeavor Global, extends its reach through diverse programs tailored to inspire, understand, support, and connect entrepreneurs globally. The campaign collaborates with local event organizers to create enduring connections, spotlight entrepreneurs, and facilitate access to resources for sustainable growth.
