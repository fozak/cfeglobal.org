People behind the Center for Entrepreneurship

Patricia Cloherty

Patricia Cloherty has been collaborating with Center for Entrepreneurship since 2002. She is recognized for her role as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at the Center for Entrepreneurship. With a distinguished career in venture investment, Patricia has garnered worldwide recognition for her relentless efforts in promoting entrepreneurship globally. Beyond her critical role at the Center for Entrepreneurship, Patricia also serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors for the U.S. Russia Investment Fund, where she has been pivotal in fostering entrepreneurial activities and forming strategic international partnerships. Patricia's tenure in the venture investment industry has been marked by a series of successful initiatives, including the creation and support of numerous high-impact programs aimed at developing entrepreneurial skills and business acumen worldwide. Her profound expertise and visionary leadership have been instrumental in steering the Center for Entrepreneurship towards achieving its mission of nurturing innovative business practices and providing essential resources to budding entrepreneurs. Patricia's contribution is not just limited to her roles in various organizations, but she also plays an influential part in mentoring and guiding new entrepreneurs, thereby ensuring a tangible and lasting impact on the global entrepreneurship landscape.
