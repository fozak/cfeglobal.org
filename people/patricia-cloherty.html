People behind the Center for Entreprenuership

Patricia Cloherty

Patricia Cloherty has been in partnership with Center for Entrepreneurship since 2003. As a member of the Advisory Board, Patricia Cloherty brings her extensive experience in venture capital and private equity to foster entrepreneurial growth and innovation. Patricia previously served as Chairman and CEO of DFJ Caspian Ventures, where she significantly contributed to the growth of early-stage companies in emerging markets. Her leadership in the venture capital industry is underscored by her role at The Partners Group, where she played a crucial role in managing private equity investments. Her distinguished career includes being a top executive at Apax Partners, one of the world's leading private equity investment groups. Through her exceptional guidance and strategic thinking, Patricia has been pivotal in steering numerous companies toward success, particularly in the technology and healthcare sectors. Patricia's involvement with the Center for Entrepreneurship allows her to leverage her vast expertise in supportive policy, financing, and practical guidance to foster sustainable entrepreneurial ecosystems globally. Her commitment to the development of entrepreneurial talent is evident in her mentorship programs and active participation in various initiatives that aim to bridge the gap between knowledge and practice. Her contribution to the Center's mission has been invaluable, fostering an environment where innovative ideas can flourish. Patricia continues to play an instrumental role in curriculum development and strategic planning, ensuring that the Center remains at the forefront of entrepreneurial education and support.
