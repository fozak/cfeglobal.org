Center for Entrepreneurship is partnering with American Chamber of Commerce (AmCham) from 2003.  

The American Chamber of Commerce is a nonprofit, nongovernmental organization . The chamber provides information, networking opportunities, business support services, and advocacy to promote a mutually beneficial bilateral business environment for the U.S. companies


Center for Entrepreneurship has partnered with AmCham to develop programs that strengthen local ecosystems around the world by connecting US entrepreneurs, ecosystem builders and community leaders.
